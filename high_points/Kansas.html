Mount Sunflower (Kansas)

Elevation: 4,041ft / 1,232m
Date Completed: April 26, 2019
Chronology: High point #15 of 50

Figure 1: Monument atop Mount Sunflower.

Figure 2: Side view of the monument atop Mount Sunflower, from farther away.

Kansas' Mount Sunflower is privately owned, but the owners have intentionally opened it to the public, and they win the award for best sense of humor about their high point. The tongue-in-cheek monument at its summit features a large metal sunflower, a mailbox register, and metal signs proclaiming the name of the 'mountain' and that it is the highest point in Kansas. There's also a metal canopy nearby with a different register and a communal library.

Like other mountain peaks, Mount Sunflower is a windy place. Sometimes I don't know how anybody living in Kansas puts up with the wind. I guess there's just no way to escape it other than to build structures.
