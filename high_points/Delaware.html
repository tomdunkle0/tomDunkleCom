Ebright Azimuth (Delaware)

Elevation: 447ft / 136m
Date Completed: May 26, 2020
Chronology: High point #27 of 50

Figure 1: Sign marking the Ebright Azimuth.

Figure 2: Bench next to sign marking the Ebright Azimuth.

Of all of the state high points, Delaware's Ebright Azimuth is the only one that is in a truly suburban environment. The high point sits along Ebright Road in Azimuth, Delaware. It's marked with a geographic marker, a sign, and a bench.

At 447 feet, it's also the second lowest state high point in the country, bested only by Florida's Britton Hill (345 feet).
