Hoye Crest (Maryland)

Elevation: 3,360ft / 1,024m
Date Completed: May 24, 2020
Chronology: High point #25 of 50

Figure 1: The view from Hoye Crest.

Figure 2: A different view, from the side while approaching Hoye Crest.

Hoye Crest requires a tiny bit of hiking, but it's an extremely easy high point to achieve. The Backbone Mountain Trailhead to the summit is actually located in West Virginia, along US Highway 219. It took us about 20 minutes to get from the trailhead to the high point.

Figure 3: The Highpointer's Bench at Hoye Crest.

Figure 4: Tom poses with the Hoye Crest Register.

The high point features a very large cairn, a High Pointer's bench, a sign documenting the history of the name, and a mailbox register for high pointers.

Along the trail were several helpful signs that had clearly been planted by the ACME company, guiding visitors to the high point.
