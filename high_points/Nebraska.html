Panorama Point (Nebraska)

Elevation: 5,427ft / 1,654m
Date Completed: April 26, 2019
Chronology: High point #16 of 50

Figure 2: The high point monument at Panorama Point.

Nebraska's Panorama Point is quite the surreal sight. Located on protected lands occupied by the American Bison, the spot boasts a Highpointer's Bench, a desk, and a stone monument. The pictures don't do it justice, but for a low rise in a field it's actually a peaceful spot with an impressive view. Panorama Point is near the intersection of Wyoming, Nebraska, and Colorado.

As I sat and took in the view, I watched two windmills in the distance that were partially overlapped from my perspective. Gradually, the windmills went in and out of sync with one another. As I stared at them, I felt that I had grown to understand Nebraskan meditation.
